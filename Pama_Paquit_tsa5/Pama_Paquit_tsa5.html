Emilie ‘Lili’ de Rochefort (エミリ・ド・ロシュフォール Emiri do Roshufōru) is the only daughter of a wealthy Monegasque oil magnate. Given her privileged background, Lili had a pampered upbringing. Four years prior to the King of Iron Fist Tournament 5, Lili was kidnapped and held for ransom. During her captivity, Lili struggled violently to free herself and successfully defeated one of her abductors, triggering a hunger for conflict in her.

Alisa Bosconovitch (Japanese: アリサ・ボスコノビッチ). Mishima Zaibatsu facility that served as a hub for the organization’s military equipment development. Alisa was created in the image of Doctor Bosconovitch's deceased daughter. Ten years prior to the first King of Iron Fist Tournament, Doctor Bosconovitch became absorbed in his work and did not notice how ill his daughter was. After being away from home for several days at work, Bosconovitch returned home to discover that his daughter had died. Consumed by grief, he isolated himself deep in Siberia and began attempting to make an immortal android replica of his daughter. He developed cold sleep technology to preserve the body of his daughter, and succeeded in creating a flexible metal alloy that was similar to human skin - however, before he could complete his work, he was captured by the KGB and forced to create robotic weapons for the Soviet (later Russian) military.

Kazuya Mishima (三島 一八 Mishima Kazuya). Kazuya was born the son of Heihachi Mishima and Kazumi Mishima and raised on the Mishima Estate, the Mishima Clan's enormous aristocratic home in Japan. Kazuya's mother loved and doted on him, as did Kazuya's grandfather, Jinpachi Mishima, who also taught him Mishima Style Fighting Karate from a young age. Kazuya's life took a turn for the worse however when he was five years old. First, his beloved grandfather disappeared (Kazuya would later discover he was imprisoned by Heihachi under the family temple, after Jinpachi failed to retake the Mishima Zaibatsu from Heihachi, who had previously launched a coup against him). Then, Kazuya's mother was killed by Heihachi. Unbeknown to Kazuya, his mother had been tasked with stopping Heihachi from going down a warmongering path at all costs. When her cause seemed lost, she fell into a series of illnesses, and was eventually taken over by a demonic alter ego which attempted to kill Heihachi.

Reina (麗奈 Reina). Reina entered the King of Iron Fist Tournament 8’s Asia Preliminaries. In the arena, she met Jin Kazama, Heihachi’s grandson, and introduced herself to him. Jin ignored her, but they were matched against one another in the semifinals. Reina knew that the Mishima Style Fighting Karate she used in battle had contributed to the awakening of Jin’s devil powers at Heihachi's hands in the past. During their fight, Jin’s powers activated, and Reina concluded that the ‘threat of death’ was key to unlocking a devil’s power. She then forfeited the match, having seen all she needed to. Later, she approached Jin in the arena again, telling him she wanted to help take down Kazuya Mishima. Jin was suspicious of her intentions, given her fighting style, but she brushed his concerns off.
