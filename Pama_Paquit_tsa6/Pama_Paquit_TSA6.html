Mario's Adventure



Mario's not alone! Luigi and Toad join his quest through vibrant jungles, arid wastelands, and even a spooky twilight realm. He'll gain fiery blasts, tanooki transformations, and the help of unlikely allies like Donkey Kong. Giant hammers smash through walls, lanterns ward off shadowy foes, and boss battles against Bowser's rambunctious Koopalings test his skills. Atop a flying fortress, Mario faces Bowser in a fiery showdown to save the princess, but the adventure doesn't end there. Mini-games, hidden secrets, and a deeper threat to the Mushroom Kingdom await!
